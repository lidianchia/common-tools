What are LCM and HCF?

Highest Common Factor (HCF/GCD)

The largest factor that divides two or more integers. For example: HCF of 12, 18, 24 is 6.

Least Common Multiple (LCM)

The smallest multiple that is divisible by two or more integers. For example: LCM of 12, 18, 24 is 72.
